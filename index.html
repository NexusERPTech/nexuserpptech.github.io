ODOO 18 · WEBSITE

Website Email Verification

Adds OTP-based email verification to the Odoo website registration flow. Prevents fake accounts and ensures every customer has a confirmed, valid email address.

  • OTP sent on signup — required before account activation
  • Configurable expiry time and resend limit
  • Works with Odoo native website portal
  • Pure OWL + Python — no frontend dependencies

ODOO 18 · ACCOUNTING

Invoice Overdue Block

Automatically blocks sales order confirmation for customers with overdue invoices. Enforces credit control at the point of sale — no manual checking needed.

  • Blocks SO confirmation on overdue accounts
  • Configurable grace period and overdue threshold
  • Clear warning showing overdue invoice details
  • Manager override with full audit trail
