Our apps

Purpose-built Odoo modules

Every module solves a real problem found in production. No bloat, no unnecessary dependencies.

ODOO 18 · WEBSITE

Website Email Verification

Adds OTP-based email verification to the Odoo website registration flow. Prevents fake accounts and ensures every customer has a confirmed email address.

  • OTP sent on signup — required before activation
  • Configurable expiry time and resend limit
  • Works with Odoo native website portal
  • Pure OWL + Python — no frontend dependencies

ODOO 18 · ACCOUNTING

Invoice Overdue Block

Automatically blocks sales order confirmation for customers with overdue invoices. Enforces credit control at the point of sale — no manual checking needed.

  • Blocks SO confirmation on overdue accounts
  • Configurable grace period and threshold
  • Clear warning showing overdue invoice details
  • Manager override with full audit trail
Odoo Apps GitHub
ODOO 18 · MANUFACTURING

Kitchen Production Tracker

A complete multi-stage production workflow for food manufacturers and bakeries — with role-based dashboards, BOM explosion, and full audit logging.

  • 6-stage pipeline: Chef → QC → Packaging → Logistics
  • Role-based dashboards per team
  • BOM explosion with phantom component support
  • CSV export and KPI filters for logistics
